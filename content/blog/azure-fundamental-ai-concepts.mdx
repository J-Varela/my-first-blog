# Knowledge check

1. You want to create a model to predict sales of ice cream based on historic data that includes daily ice cream sales totals and weather measurements. Which Azure service should you use?

- Azure Machine Learning

2. You work for a wildlife sanctuary and are considering using Ai to identify bird species from images. Which AI service should you use to prototype your idea?

- Azure AI Vision

3. A predictive app provides audio output for visually impaired users. Which principle of Responsible AI is reflected here?

- Iclusiveness

# Summary

In this module, you learned about the fundamental concepts of AI and how to use Azure AI services. You learned about the AI lifecycle, the types of AI, and the principles of Responsible AI. You also learned how to use Azure AI services to build AI solutions.

Artificial Intelligence enables the creation of powerful solutions to many kinds of problems. AI systems can exhibit human characteristics to analyze the world around theme, make predictions or inferences, and act on them in ways that we could only imagine a short time ago.
